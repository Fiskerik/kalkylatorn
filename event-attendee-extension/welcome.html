Prospect In Event Workflow

Follow this workflow to collect LinkedIn event attendees and export them for CRM import.

  1. Login to LinkedIn.
  2. Go to an event and press Attend.
  3. Go to the Networking tab to see attendees.
  4. Scroll down to See all.
  5. Open the ProspectIn side panel and press the Extract Attendees button to populate the list.
  6. Download CSV or PDF in your preferred format.
  7. Import the file to your CRM.

Tip: Keep this page pinned while onboarding your team so everyone follows the same process.
